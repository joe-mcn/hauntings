Simpson Ghost Hunters

The Simpson Ghost Hunters investigate haunted locations and paranormal activity around the Simpson College campus. These experiences are real and can be backed up with witnesses and evidence. Our hunters have spent hours on lockdown inside these locations and are trained to defend themselves from the paranormal. Due to the unpredictable nature of these anomalous creatures, we highly advise that you never try this alone.

Meet the Team


The team was formed in 2017 when the 4 members shared a supernatural experience in College Hall together. Upon uncovering supernatural entities occupying the campus the 4 key members agreed to team. Coincidentally, all four members have had previous experience with the paranormal. Supernatural forces and it takes a group of highly trained and skilled people experienced in the supernatural arts to get ot the bottom of it. Above all, our mission is to keep the campus safe and raise awareness so our fellow students won't fall to the paranormal entities.


Tristan "Big Bill" Bocook


Tristan started off as any regular joe until his sister Mika took her friends to Villisca Axe Murder House. What was supposed to be a trip that lasted a few days turned into a missing case where Mika and her friends haven't returned from for a few years. Upon arrival in the house Tristan discovered her glasses and a cracked phone stuck at the time 11pm which is the same time a father axe-murdered his whole family. Swearing to uncover the truth Tristan went to a Simpson college where he heard of a certain group bent on uncovering the paranormal.


Michael "Emu" Roberts


Michael was a top of the line prodigy specializing in computers. While on a full ride to Simpson Michael set up shop and life was normal. Only one problem, in his eyes life for Michael was not normal. When Michael was a youg lad he lived in a house where hardworking author died at his typewriter. In the middle of the night michael would hear the clicking of a type writer and one day he woke up in the dead of night. In front of Michael's eyes was the silouhette of an old man hunched over a typewriter. Michael feared him until he found his hobby in computers and upon his arrival at simpson he formed Simpson Hauntings to uncover the supernatural.


Jared "Mallen" Gast


Jared was born and raised on Morgan Wallen spent his youth bumping every cd he and his father could get their hands on. One night when Jared was in his room listening to Morgan Wallen on his ipod, he randomly looked out his window and staring back at him was a lady dressed in Victorian Era clothes. She seemed to be speaking to him but upon opening his window he heard an out of tune orchestral voice greeting him. When he rubbed his eyes, she was out of sight. Suddenly, a voice whispered "How do you do?". Jared now dedicates his free time at Simpson rooting out the supernatural to ensure the safety of his peers.


Joe "Graves" McNally


Joe spent his years with his father digging graves for the people of his hometown but on one unfortunate night Joe had to prepare a grave by himself. He thoguht he had heard voices of children playing nearby. Joe looked at the area around him and saw nothing. When he resumed his work he heard an old man yell "GIVE BACK EVERYTHING YOU'VE STOLEN" and in front of him was the silhouette of an old man. Joe ran as fast he could and swore to himself that he would go after supernatural forces to prove he was crazy to his old man. Upon arriving at simpson he went to College Hall he heard the voice of young women upon trying to retrieve something he left. It was at that fateful moment he bumped into Michael and Jared. The 3 formed a Paranormal hunting group known as Simpson Ghost Hunters.
